Calisthenics is Awesome

Calisthenics is a great way to gain strength and control over you bodyweight, and also to do some cool tricks too!

Dominate your whole body

Use push exercises to build your chest, triceps and shoulders

Do pull exercises like pull ups to have an amazing back and biceps

Do all types of squats to build great legs and glutes

Build stability and strength in your core doing planks and other core exercises

"Calisthenics transforms your body using just your weight, turning strength into art. With every movement, you sculpt not only muscle but also resilience and grace"

— Frank Medrano
